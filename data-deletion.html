Data Deletion Instructions

If you want to delete your data associated with AdhamApplication, follow these steps:

Option 1: Remove the App from Facebook

You can delete your data by removing the app from your Facebook account:

  1. Go to Facebook App Settings.
  2. Find and select AdhamApplication.
  3. Click "Remove" to delete the app and its associated data.
